# Spine

Everything reduces to one five-part loop:

1. **Perceive** — the sensor head turns thermal/radar/camera into clean events.
2. **[[Remember]]** — events accumulate into a pattern of life. *Behavioral Analysis* — the heart.
3. **[[Reason]]** — the local LLM weighs *now* against *normal* and decides what matters.
4. **[[Act]]** — Home Assistant carries out the decision.
5. **Interface** — voice-first, and [[Consent and Gestures|gesture]]-confirmed; the window into the loop.

## Learning by friction
Homie optimizes for **needing you less over time**.
- Silence = approval · Reversal = correction · Repeated manual action = missing pattern · Spoken remark = strongest.
- Success metric: the *declining rate of corrections*.

Built on the [[Bus]]; structured as [[Core and Tiles]].
