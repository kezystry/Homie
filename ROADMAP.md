# Roadmap

Incremental build order. Each phase ships something usable and is a foundation
for the next.

| Phase | Focus | Goal |
|-------|-------|------|
| 1 | **Sensor head** | Bring up the Schauender Kopf — sensors stream over a single PoE cable. |
| 2 | **Enclosure** | Ceiling-mounted unit indistinguishable from a smoke detector. |
| 3 | **Edge perception** | Hailo-accelerated detection and thermal/radar fusion into structured events. |
| 4 | **Behavioral core** | Model the household's pattern of life; expose it as the shared API. |
| 5 | **Control** | Home Assistant actuation driven by presence and learned routines. |
| 6 | **Pillars** | Layer on Security, Personal, Kitchen, and Werkstatt. |

Status: Phase 1 in progress.
